In the midst of life’s turmoil, Lord Krishna whispers through the Gītā that true devotion is not about grand offerings, but about purity of heart and unwavering surrender. He teaches that even the simplest gift—a leaf, a flower, a fruit, or a drop of water—when offered with genuine love, gladdens His heart and opens the door to spiritual liberation bhagavad-gita.org gitauniversity.in. Krishna assures us that performing our duties with equanimity—without clinging to success or fearing failure—is the path of yoga, the art of living in inner peace Hosh Yoga Wikipedia.
He calls us to devotion through steadfast faith, encouraging us to think of Him, worship Him, and surrender every action to Him—promising that those who do so will surely reach Him with a heart free from fear Hosh Yoga gitauniversity.in
Thus, devotion becomes a living offering: an everyday prayer woven into every moment, transforming the ordinary into the divine.
